Keto Pizza Puffs

This delicious snack offers a different way to enjoy pizza puff. Instead of the usual deep-fried dough, this one is made similar to a muffin. Perfect to be eaten during snack time!

Ingredients

  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 1 cup Almond Flour
  • 1 cup shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • Â½ tsp Dried Oregano
  • Â¼ tsp White Pepper
  • 1 tbsp Tomato Sauce
  • Â¼ cup Cooked Ham, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F. Lightly brush a silicon muffin pan with oil.
  2. Beat eggs and tomato sauce in a bowl.
  3. Add cheese, almond flour, baking powder, oregano, and white pepper. Stir until well blended.
  4. Fold in the ham.
  5. Divide the batter into the muffin pan and bake for 15 minutes.
