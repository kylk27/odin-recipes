Homemade French Fries

allumette-cut deep-fried potatoes, originating from either Belgium or France.

Ingredients:

  • 2 or 3 Large potatoes
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt and Pepper

How to Cook:

  1. Slice the potatoes 1/2 inch thick
  2. Soak them in cold water for atleast an hour or overnight.(Longer=crispier fries)
  3. Rinse them twice with cold water and pat them dry.
  4. Heat oil to 300 degrees. Fry them in about 6 batches for 5-6minutes. Don't overcrowd them by placing too many at a time, they won't be as crispy. Use a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel.
  5. Double fry the fries. Increase heat to 400 degrees. Fry in batches until golden brown, about 5 minutes.
  6. Place them on paper towels and sprinkle immediately with salt. Serve with ketchup.
